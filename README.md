# No Sweat Enforcement & Punishment Policy

This Enforcement & Punishment Policy defines how rule violations are addressed within the **No Sweat** community.  
It exists to ensure fairness, consistency, and accountability while maintaining a respectful and competitive environment.

All members are subject to this Policy.

---

## 1. Enforcement Principles

All enforcement actions are guided by the following principles:

- Fairness and consistency
- Proportional response to violations
- Protection of the community
- Staff discretion based on context and severity

Not all violations receive warnings. Severe misconduct may result in immediate action.

---

## 2. Types of Enforcement Actions

### 2.1 Warning
A formal notice issued for minor or first-time violations.

- May be verbal or written
- Logged by staff for reference
- Repeated warnings may escalate to further action

---

### 2.2 Timeout (Temporary Communication Restriction)
A temporary restriction from text and/or voice communication.

**Purpose:**
- De-escalation
- Prevent disruption
- Provide time to cool down

**Typical Duration:**
- 5 minutes to 24 hours

**Common Reasons:**
- Spam or flooding
- Minor toxicity
- Ignoring staff instructions

---

### 2.3 Suspension (Temporary Membership Restriction)
A temporary removal of access to No Sweat platforms.

**Purpose:**
- Address repeated or moderate violations
- Protect community integrity

**Typical Duration:**
- 24 hours to 30 days

**Common Reasons:**
- Repeated rule violations
- Harassment or disrespect
- Abuse of privileges
- Failure to comply with staff decisions

Suspended members may lose rank or privileges upon return.

---

### 2.4 Kick (Immediate Removal)
Immediate removal from a No Sweat platform without a permanent ban.

**Purpose:**
- Stop ongoing disruption
- Act as a final warning

**Common Reasons:**
- Severe disruption
- Escalating conflict
- Refusal to comply with staff

Rejoining after a kick is not guaranteed and may require staff approval.

---

### 2.5 Ban (Permanent Removal)
Permanent removal from all No Sweat platforms.

**Purpose:**
- Protect the community from severe or repeated violations

**Common Reasons:**
- Cheating or exploiting
- Hate speech or threats
- Severe harassment or discrimination
- Impersonation of staff
- Repeated violations after prior enforcement
- Circumventing enforcement actions

Bans are permanent unless explicitly overturned by leadership.

---

## 3. Escalation Process

Enforcement actions typically follow this progression:

Warning  
→ Timeout  
→ Suspension  
→ Kick  
→ Ban  

Staff may bypass steps depending on severity.

---

## 4. Rank & Privilege Impact

- Enforcement actions may result in rank removal or demotion
- Elite or leadership roles may be revoked immediately
- Access to restricted channels may be removed permanently

Rank status does not grant immunity from enforcement.

---

## 5. Appeals Process

Appeals may be submitted only under the following conditions:

- New or relevant evidence is provided
- The appeal is respectful and factual
- Appeals are submitted through approved channels

Staff decisions on appeals are final.

---

## 6. Staff Authority

- Staff have full authority to enforce this Policy
- Enforcement decisions are not subject to public discussion
- Harassment or pressure toward staff regarding enforcement is prohibited

---

## 7. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated at any time.  
Continued participation in No Sweat constitutes acceptance of the current version.

---

## 8. Enforcement Standard

No Sweat enforces rules to protect the community — not to punish unnecessarily.

Respect the rules.  
Respect the process.  
Respect the community.

**No Sweat — fair play, fair enforcement.**
